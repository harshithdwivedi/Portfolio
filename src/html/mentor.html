About my Mentoring experience

I believe that sharing is caring, and even more so when you talk about technology.

Apart from my work, which is majorly focused towards teaching and mentoring people, I also try to find ways to mentor and offer my help students and developers with their queries related to both Android and career in my spare time.

Currently I'm working with Udacity as an Android, Machine Learning and Digital Marketing mentor where i Provide direct support and feedback to students enrolled in Udacity's Nanodegree program.

I'm also a mentor for Google Summer of Code and Google Code In with FOSSASIA where I help students coming to Open Source for the first time.

In the past, I've been associated with Google's Applied CS with Android program in which I taught CS Fundamentals and Algorithms to college students using Android as a Platform.

I was also a mentor for the MIT Martin Trust Center for the MIT Launch Clubs program which focused on teaching entrepreneurship to college students.

Be sure to give me a shout out if you or someone you know needs any help with figuring out what's next or what to do in your career and I'll be happy to help as much as I can!
